TextReview2StarRating

TextReview2StarRating is a service that transforms a text review to a rating in the form of [1-5] stars.

In order to train our models we use the dataset provided from Yelp containing reviews for venues. We trained several Machine Learning models like Naive Bayes and SVM that lead us in an accuracy of 59% at most. We then tried the Neural Networks approach by training a Convolutional Neural Network and two Recurrent Neural Networks: an LSTM and an LSTM with an attention layer.

The CNN reached to an accuracy of 61.5%, the LSTM to an accuracy of 62.5 and the LSTM with an attention layer to an accuracy of 63.1%. There are a lot of parameters that we can still experiment with and that they may lead us to an even better result.

Additionally, on the evaluation we performed we used a confusion matrix whitch proved that 85% of our models mistake are neighbouring classes. In other words, our model could wrongly evaluate a 5-star text review as a 4-star one, but rarely it would have evaluated the same review as an 1-star one. And even these mistakes were happening in reviews that wasn't clear what they meant.

In this page you will find a lightweight example of a small model we trained for presentation purposes. Additionally, in our project we provide a Python implementation of a RESTful API that can exploit the full potentional of our model.
